Text: Showcasing undergraduate 2D design work from the Corcoran School of the Arts & Design at the George Washington University, corcoran:Design provides users with a canvas of thumbnails to explore. Individual projects contain CDN-hosted images, brief descriptions, and links to a student’s online presence. Additionally, a secondary info page summarizes academic structure and culture. Employed for recruiting over a one-year duration following iOS App Store certification.
